While every animal at the Aquarium helps to educate the public on their wild counterparts, there are some with that specific job! These animals, called ambassador animals, do not have a public facing habitat but instead help educate the public on their wild counterparts through presentations and amphitheater talks.

Who's Who

Our roster of Ambassadors is ever growing! Currently the Aquarium has “Pip” the Pelican, “Wilson” the barn owl, two Western Pond Turtles, “Cuff” and “Link”, “Rocky” the Rubber Boa, Painted Turtle “Smudge”, the American Kestrel, “Hazel”, and Banana Slug “Pudding”.

About us

SUBSCRIBE NOW ABOUT OREGON COAST AQUARIUM Since opening in 1992, the Aquarium has been a premier attraction on central coast and an enduring advocate for the health of our ocean. For over three decades this 501(c)3 non-profit organization has supported marine wildlife and fostered environmental stewardship through its education programs, community partnerships, and rehabilitation efforts. Accredited by the Association of Zoos & Aquariums, is consistently ranked as one of the top 10 aquariums in the P.k, providing visitors with opportunities to connect with 15,000 animals spanning 300 different species. The Aquarium is located at aptech nn OR. Learn more about the Aquarium and its vital work at aquarium. Follow us on Facebook or Twitter for the latest updates.
