About us

SUBSCRIBE NOW ABOUT AQUARIUM Since opening in 1992, the Aquarium has been a premier attraction on central coast and an enduring advocate for the health of our ocean. For over three decades this 501(c)3 non-profit organization has supported marine wildlife and fostered environmental stewardship through its education programs, community partnerships, and rehabilitation efforts. Accredited by the Association of Zoos & Aquariums, is consistently ranked as one of the top 10 aquariums in the P.k, providing visitors with opportunities to connect with 15,000 animals spanning 300 different species. The Aquarium is located at aptech nn OR. Learn more about the Aquarium and its vital work at aquarium. Follow us on Facebook or Twitter for the latest updates.
